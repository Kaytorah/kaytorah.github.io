CROSS RIVER STATE

Cross river state is located in south souh Nigeria, its a peaceful, clean and hospitable state
and top tourist location in the country and Africa as well. It's capital city Calabar is famed for
it's street carnival, which is the largest in Africa as well as delicious native delicacies.
A visit to Cross River state is definitely a visit too remember, amazing places to visit, amazing
things to see, things to do, and beautiful cultural displays. Places to visit in Cross-River state includes:

OBUDU MOUNTAIN RESORT

Also known as Obudu cattle ranch, it is one of Africa's finnest and most interesting and spectacular
tourist destination. It is located in the highlands and deep tropical forest of Cross-River state. With a
rich natural vegetaion and a pituresque view and a beautiful terrain.

Sights to see:

  • Beautiful mountain view with the fogs.
  • The cable car.
  • Large cattles.

Things to do:

  • Attend a bonfire night.
  • Take a walk on the canopy walkway.
  • Take a ride in the cable cars.
  • Bird watching with binoculars.

AGBOKIM WATER-FALL

The Agbokim waterfall is located in Etung, Cross-River state,
and also a tourist location,the waterfall is made up of seven streams
with a cascade of fresh water plunging down the high cliff into the
tropical rainforest, It is a beautiful sight to behold.The beauty of this natural wonder
bring tourists from far and near, the sound of the waterfall is amazing and calming.

Things to do:

  • Take a swim bath.
  • Take pictures and videos of the enviroment.
  • Appreciate the wonder and beauty of nature.

CALABAR STREET CARNIVAL

It is an anual carnival held in the capital city of Cross-River state, as well as an activity
to make christmas day celebration. The carnival consists of several bandswith their amazing
costumes, these bands parade the streets of calabar with their flambouyant colorful costumes
and perfomances. The events include musical performances from both local and and international artists.

Things to see:

  • Beautiful ladies in amazing costumes.
  • Highly entertaining performances from local and international bands.
  • Celebrities/Famous personas.

Things to do:

  • Take pictures of and with different bands.
  • Party, dance and have fun.

Cross-river is home to known delicious native delicacies which includes; Afang soup, Edikan-ikong soup,Ekpankukwo etc.
